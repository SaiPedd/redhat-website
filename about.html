About the Program

The Red Hat High School Internship Program provides a unique opportunity for high school students to gain hands-on experience across various aspects of a leading open-source company. This section highlights my weekly activities, insights, and experiences throughout the internship.

Weekly Highlights

Week 1

This week focuses on essential skills and strategies to prepare for future careers. Activities include workshops on confidence, networking, and professional presentation.

Highlights of Week 1
  • Introduction to Red Hat's culture and values
  • Met with mentors and team members
  • Participated in initial training sessions and workshops on professional skills
  • Office tour and setting up workstations
  • Engaged in team-building activities and networking

Week 2

This week dives into the world of technology and innovation through hands-on activities and workshops, job shadowing sessions, and interactive learning labs.

Highlights of Week 2
  • Participated in job shadowing sessions to gain insights into various tech roles
  • Engaged in learning labs on web development, GitHub, UX design, and pattern recognition
  • Attended workshops and talks on tech careers, hardware, and artificial intelligence
  • Connected with mentors and professionals to understand workflows and career paths

Week 3

This week focuses on exploring the pivotal role of data in the digital age through engaging sessions on data, marketing, and advanced applications of artificial intelligence (AI).

Highlights of Week 3
  • Participated in job shadowing sessions to gain insights into various roles involving data
  • Engaged in info sessions on topics such as data analytics, marketing, and communications
  • Attended learning labs on data analytics and AI foundations
  • Joined interactive sessions on unexpected engineering uses for data and AI engineer panel discussions

Week 4

This week focuses on understanding Red Hat's culture, working on final projects, and learning about the company's values and practices.

Highlights of Week 4
  • Worked on final projects, including "Day in the Life" and group portfolios
  • Scheduled meetings with selected Red Hat employees to gain insights
  • Engaged in activities to learn about Red Hat's culture and values
  • Attended info sessions about AI and other advanced topics
